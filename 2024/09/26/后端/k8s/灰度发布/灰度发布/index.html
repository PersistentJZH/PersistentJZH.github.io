基于deployment的灰度发布

  1. 部署旧版本deployment/service
  2. 部署canary版本deployment/service
    1. 分阶段扩容到desire quantity
      kubectl scale –replicas=1 deployment/dummy-sdu-dummy-cluster-222
      kubectl scale –replicas=2 deployment/dummy-sdu-dummy-cluster-222
      kubectl scale –replicas=3 deployment/dummy-sdu-dummy-cluster-222
    2. kubectl delete service my-deployment-111 && kubectl delete deployment my-deployment-111
    3. 删除service/ep canary label （kubectl label service dummy-sdu-dummy-cluster-222 version- –overwrite）
